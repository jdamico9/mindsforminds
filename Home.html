Minds for Minds


Hi, welcome to the official home page of Minds for Minds, a nonprofit organized by Jameson D'Amico! This organization is dedicated to providing services to teenagers looking for mental health treatment, as well as helping them find the appropriate mental health resources. For more information about the organization and what we do, please click go to the "About" section. To get in touch, please go to the contact section.
